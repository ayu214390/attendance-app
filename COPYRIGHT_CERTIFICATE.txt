5. Description of the Work
------------------------------------------------------------
This software is an attendance and payroll management application
designed for small retail businesses.

Its main features include:
- Attendance check-in and check-out
- Break and meal tracking
- Monthly payroll calculation
- CSV (Excel-compatible) export

This software was developed as a practical project and is currently
published as a demo / portfolio version.
No real shop data, personal information, or salary records are included.
